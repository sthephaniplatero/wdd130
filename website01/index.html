Flavors from the center of the country!

Linda Vista Garden Restaurant (Santa Tecla, San Salvador)

A beautiful place located in El Boquerón Volcan de San Salvador, to enjoy a rich afternoon observing the beautiful places and feeling the rich climate tasting delicious delicacies of international cuisine among gardens, clouds, music, flowers, and the best view of San Salvador. International breakfasts with a Salvadoran touch, high quality cuts of meat with prices ranging from breakfast $6-$8 and lunch and dinner $15-$20 per person.

Las Tres Piedras Restaurant (San Francisco Chinameca)

A spectacular restaurant, relatively hidden among the wonders that nature offers us, with an incredible view of Lake Ilopango, and surprisingly also of the Pacific coast; A whole genius just 25 minutes from the city. The food is a delight for the palate, it is really haute cuisine with delicious Salvadoran food and the natural and exquisite flavor of a good grill and an excellent grill chef. Prices per person between $15-$18

Casa Campo Mirador Restaurant (Planes de Renderos)

PUPUSAS the typical Salvadoran dish and Casa Campo Mirador is the perfect place, located on Planes de Renderos street, a magical place with a mountain cabin design that makes it stand out from the rest of the buildings that surround it. It has a Nice façade that arouses curiosity and upon entering you can see the tables and in the background a spectacular view of the city and where you can see a part of Lake Ilopango. Typical breakfasts, Mexican food, barbecues and the exquisite pupusas with their specialty pupusa loca make the perfect menu to enjoy this beautiful place! Prices between $5 and $18 dollars per person.
