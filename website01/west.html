Jardin de Celeste (Ahuachapán)

Restaurant with a unique design, surrounded by beautiful gardens, a nursery that houses a wide variety of plants, an orchid garden and our coffee plantation on the Ruta de las Flores at Km 94 1/2 of the road that leads from Concepción de Ataco to Apaneca in the department of Ahuachapan. Food made in a rustic way, freshly baked bread in its clay oven and a floral decoration that makes this one of the best restaurants in the west of Salvador.

Volcanes Bistro Café.(Izalco)

Food in the clouds! Los Volcanes Bistró Café was born with the hope of the owners of sharing their pride in being Salvadorans in one of the best places in our country with the most impressive views accompanied by a varied menu for the whole family with typical and foreign dishes, surrounded by nature and the imposing volcanoes of Izalco and Santa Ana in a unique environment that can only be found at the height of the clouds, as well as sharing our passion and pride in coffee from El Salvador as one of the best in the world, all this in one magical place. El Cerro Verde

La Pampa Coatepeque (Santa Ana)

Among the mountains that surround Lake Coatepeque, in Santa Ana, is La Pampa Coatepeque, a restaurant that not only offers a pleasant experience in terms of flavor and service, but also an impressive experience, a place that has managed to be recognized for its quality. of their dishes and competitive prices. Among the specialties offered to visitors is a wide variety of imported cuts of meat and different seafood cooked on the grill while you can take a tour and take the respective souvenir photograph in its "giant chair", with the wooden frame or with the lake in the background with a spectacular view.
