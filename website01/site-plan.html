Purpose

Content

Home page

El Salvador "Pulgarsito de America" ​​be part of the trend by visiting this small country but with great cultural diversity and natural wonders, climate, security and accessibility that many countries would envy. Between lakes, mountains, beaches and volcanoes that you can visit with a 90-minute difference between each one, join us to discover the most delicious gastronomy of top and unique places in this country with an incredible price that makes you fall in love with this small country more and more. find magical and perfect places to eat!

Flavors from the center of the country!

Linda Vista Garden Restaurant (Santa Tecla, San Salvador) A beautiful place located in El Boquerón Volcan de San Salvador, to enjoy a rich afternoon observing the beautiful places and feeling the rich climate tasting delicious delicacies of international cuisine among gardens, clouds, music, flowers, and the best view of San Salvador. International breakfasts with a Salvadoran touch, high quality cuts of meat with prices ranging from breakfast $6-$8 and lunch and dinner $15-$20 per person.

Las Tres Piedras Restaurant (San Francisco Chinameca) A spectacular restaurant, relatively hidden among the wonders that nature offers us, with an incredible view of Lake Ilopango, and surprisingly also of the Pacific coast; A whole genius just 25 minutes from the city. The food is a delight for the palate, it is really haute cuisine with delicious Salvadoran food and the natural and exquisite flavor of a good grill and an excellent grill chef. Prices per person between $15-$18

Casa Campo Mirador Restaurant (Planes de Renderos) PUPUSAS the typical Salvadoran dish and Casa Campo Mirador is the perfect place, located on Planes de Renderos street, a magical place with a mountain cabin design that makes it stand out from the rest of the buildings that surround it. It has a Nice façade that arouses curiosity and upon entering you can see the tables and in the background a spectacular view of the city and where you can see a part of Lake Ilopango. Typical breakfasts, Mexican food, barbecues and the exquisite pupusas with their specialty pupusa loca make the perfect menu to enjoy this beautiful place! Prices between $5 and $18 dollars per person.

Flavors in the west

Jardin de Celeste (Ahuachapán) Restaurant with a unique design, surrounded by beautiful gardens, a nursery that houses a wide variety of plants, an orchid garden and our coffee plantation on the Ruta de las Flores at Km 94 1/2 of the road that leads from Concepción de Ataco to Apaneca in the department of Ahuachapan. Food made in a rustic way, freshly baked bread in its clay oven and a floral decoration that makes this one of the best restaurants in the west of Salvador.

Los Volcanes Bistro Café.(Izalco) Food in the clouds! Los Volcanes Bistró Café was born with the hope of the owners of sharing their pride in being Salvadorans in one of the best places in our country with the most impressive views accompanied by a varied menu for the whole family with typical and foreign dishes, surrounded by nature and the imposing volcanoes of Izalco and Santa Ana in a unique environment that can only be found at the height of the clouds, as well as sharing our passion and pride in coffee from El Salvador as one of the best in the world, all this in one magical place. El Cerro Verde

La Pampa Coatepeque (Santa Ana) Among the mountains that surround Lake Coatepeque, in Santa Ana, is La Pampa Coatepeque, a restaurant that not only offers a pleasant experience in terms of flavor and service, but also an impressive experience, a place that has managed to be recognized for its quality. of their dishes and competitive prices. Among the specialties offered to visitors is a wide variety of imported cuts of meat and different seafood cooked on the grill while you can take a tour and take the respective souvenir photograph in its "giant chair", with the wooden frame or with the lake in the background with a spectacular view.

Flavors in the east

Casa 1800 (Suchitoto) Casa 1800 Suchitoto is a charming restaurant surrounded by nature in the town of Suchitoto with an incredible view of Lake Suchitlan with a vintage decoration that allows you to feel in a magical corner. It has delicious dishes but the specialty is pizza with typical Salvadoran flavors and ingredients such as loroco and chorizo ​​and drinks such as horchata and tropical fruit soft drinks. The place besides being a restaurant is also a hostel where you will find a warm and comfortable place to enjoy a special night. Prices per person are breakfast between $6-$8 and lunch between $15-$20

Maribel Restaurant (La Union) Another incredible thing for which Salvador is very well known are its beaches and the best are in the East of the country. Maribel Restaurant is one of the restaurants with a paradisiacal view of the Gulf of Fonseca, making this place the right environment for enjoy the delicious Salvadoran seafood. With buffet options and also dishes such as seafood platters, seafood cocktails, lobster and many more dishes, this restaurant is the perfect place to combine excellent food and an incredible view. The day pass that includes lunch costs $20.

Hotel & Restaurant Bahia del Sol (La Paz) A tropical paradise, this is a perfect place with two environments: the beach and the estuary with one of the best locations, one of the best beaches in the country. It has the all-inclusive option for the $25 pass for breakfast and lunch. This It is a hotel known for its fishing competitions in which they are held during various periods of the year since the country allows you to enjoy a pleasant climate conducive to fishing.
